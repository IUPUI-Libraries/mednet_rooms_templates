From October 1 to January 31 study rooms may not be booked and use is reserved for residency interviews.

The Ruth Lilly Medical Library has 8 study rooms on the second floor. It is the responsibility of the room users to make reservations in advance to ensure 1st priority. Please review our study room policies before booking. Faculty, staff and students at IU and IU School of Medicine can also reserve Room 303. Current room policies apply.

Study and Conference Room Policies:

  • Study rooms and conference rooms may be reserved in advance, but if the rooms are not in use or reserved, they are first-come first-serve.
  • Please refrain from moving furniture.
  • Please dispose of trash properly.
  • Abandonment of personal belongs does not constitute a room reservation.
  • Alert circulation staff to remove items left in rooms.
  • Lockers are available on second floor.
  • Dry erase markers/erasers are available for checkout at the circulation desk.
